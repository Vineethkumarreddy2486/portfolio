V23 Healthcare Platform - Virinchi Limited Technologies

V23 is a comprehensive healthcare management platform built at Virinchi Limited Technologies, divided into two separate applications — Provider App and Patient App. In the Provider App, clinics, pharmacies, and diagnostic centers can register their facilities and manage services at branch level. Providers can configure branch-level settings, manage staff, handle facility details, and track billing and invoices across multiple branches. In the Patient App, patients can search and book doctor appointments with real-time slot selection and availability checks, place prescription orders digitally, and manage their complete billing history.
